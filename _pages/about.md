# CHSLT — Center for Holographic Studies & Laser micro‑mechaTronics

The metrological applications at the CHSLT concentrate on **holographic interferometry**, **laser speckle metrology**, **fiber optic sensors**, **analytical and computational modeling** of structural behavior under static as well as dynamic loading conditions, and other areas of current interest.

In these measurements, special effort is made to develop effective means for **computer-aided quantitative analysis** of experimental data** and to relate these quantitative analyses to theoretical results.

---

## Research Areas

- Holographic interferometry  
- Laser speckle metrology  
- Fiber optic sensing  
- Structural dynamics  
- Computational modeling  
- Optical metrology
